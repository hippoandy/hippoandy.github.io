2015.06.15

The 2015 Interntional Conference on Social Business Development and Mangement @ NCU

The 2015 Interntional Conference (taken on 20150615)

This conference was host by the Yunus Social Business Center, NCU, held on June 15, 2015, which was the mid-term of our project.

At this conference, the 8 student groups of the Yunus Social Business Center, NCU (including us) had to present our project result. On that day, me and one of my team member were going to present on stage, which made me feeling stressful and nervous since I didn’t have many experience to present in a big auditorium.

I Presented at The 2015 Interntional Conference (taken on 20150615)

They also required us to make a poster to show the overview of our project.

Our Poster for The 2015 Interntional Conference (taken on 20150615)

At the end of the conference, they were going to give the prizes and certificates to 3 of the 8 student groups. Unfortunately, we did not get any award. The project of the other groups is and biannual one. Therefore, at this time, their projects were totally completed. Since our project required 1 year to finish, we did not have enough result to show the jurors.

Our Team at The 2015 Interntional Conference (taken on 20150615)

2015.12.19

MIS Carnival @ NCU

The MIS Carnival was a show held by the Department of Information Management, NCU. This show was a day for all the students who participated in the sample project to share the experience and present the final result of the project.

All the groups owned a booth to demo the project to the professors.

Our Team at The MIS Carnival (taken on 20151219)
